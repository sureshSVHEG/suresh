Tamil Nadu is known for its diverse and delicious cuisine. Some popular dishes include dosa, idli, sambar, rasam, biryani, and various types of chutneys and curries. The cuisine is characterized by its use of rice, lentils, and spices, and it offers a wide range of vegetarian and non-vegetarian options.

Highlights of TN cuisine include milkshakes, barbecue, and a wide range of fried foods. Many quintessential TN dishes are unique takes on food originally from other culinary traditions, including pizza, hot dogs, and Tex-Mex.
